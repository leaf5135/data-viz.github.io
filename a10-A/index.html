Reason why this chart is much better and clear: This sequential paired bar chart is a clear and effective way to represent data compared to a pie
chart. By displaying imports and exports of goods side by side, organized by type and year with a dropdown menu, it enhances comprehension. The use of
a sequential color scale aids understanding of data size, even without directly comparing bar heights. It adheres to principles like data
transformation and Gestalt principles, ensuring clarity. Annotations highlight the key detail of the chart, and the source is prominently cited below, adding credibility.
